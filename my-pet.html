My pet

My pet is a cat: Bella. She is my best friend

How I met Bella

Bella was my aunts pet and I happened to be so close to my aunt. So, I could visit my aunt every often. Bella and I got fond of each other such that she would cling on me whenever it was time for me to leave.

Aunty loved Bella so much and she always wanted happiness for her. She had realized that Bella always wanted to be around me. She decided to give Bella to me.

It's been two years now and we are still friends with Bella. Here are some of the things we do together:

  • We play together
  • We jog together
  • And sometimes we even sleep together

Bella is the best thing that ever happened to me. Thank you aunt for gifting me with her.
